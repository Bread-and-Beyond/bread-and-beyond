title: "Chocolate Chip Cookies"
category: "desserts"
favorite: true
tags: ["cookies", "chocolate", "dessert", "classic"]
image: "/bread-and-beyond/images/recipes/chocolate-chip-cookies.jpg"
dietary: "Dairy"
prepTime: "15 min"
cookTime: "12 min"
servings: "24 cookies"
publishDate: 2026-07-07
ingredientGroups:
  - items:
      - "2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
      - "1 tsp baking soda"
      - "1 tsp salt"
      - "1 cup unsalted butter, softened"
      - "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
      - "3/4 cup brown sugar"
      - "2 large eggs"
      - "2 tsp vanilla extract"
      - "2 cups chocolate chips"
steps:
  - "Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt."
  - "Cream butter with both sugars until light and fluffy."
  - "Beat in eggs one at a time, then vanilla."
  - "Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined."
  - "Fold in chocolate chips."
  - "Chill dough at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor)."
  - "Scoop onto a lined baking sheet, spaced apart."
  - "Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10–12 minutes until edges are golden."
  - "Cool on the pan 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack."
