dietary: "Parve"
prepTime: "10 min"
cookTime: "18 min"
servings: "1 sheet pan"
publishDate: 2026-07-07
ingredientGroups:
  - items:
      - "360g water, around 86°F"
      - "4g instant yeas"
      - "25g olive oil"
      - "10g granulated sugar"
      - "450g bread flour"
      - "12g salt"
      - "¼ cup olive oil, for the pan and topping"
      - "Flaky salt, for topping"
steps:
  - "In a large bowl, combine the water, instant yeast, 25g olive oil, sugar, bread flour, and salt. Stir until combined."
  - "Wet your hand and mix the dough for 2–3 minutes. Cover and let proof for 15 minutes."
  - "Build strength by pulling up the sides of the dough and folding them over 4–5 times, then slap and fold the dough into a ball 12–15 times."
  - "Cover and let rise for 45 minutes."
  - "Add ¼ cup olive oil to a cast-iron pan. Place the dough inside, oil the top, then dimple and gently spread out the dough."
  - "Let rise for 45 minutes."
  - "Sprinkle with flaky salt."
  - "Bake at 500°F for 15–18 minutes."
